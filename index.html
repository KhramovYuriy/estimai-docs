How it works

From issue to calibrated estimate in four steps

Load an Epic or issue

EstimAI pulls the description, acceptance criteria and sub-tasks from Jira — Scrum, Kanban, team- or company-managed.

Add context

Drop in design screenshots, a Figma link, grooming notes or linked Confluence specs. All optional, all powerful.

Get explainable estimates

AI estimates each task across complexity, effort and uncertainty — anchored to your team's completed work.

Run Planning Poker

Record the team's actual points. EstimAI tracks accuracy and recalibrates for next sprint.

Per-task breakdown

FAQ

Good questions

How much does it cost?

EstimAI is free and unlimited — every feature included. Because it's bring-your-own-key, you pay only your own OpenAI or Anthropic usage; EstimAI itself charges nothing. (Paid team plans may arrive in a future release.)

Why bring-your-own-key (BYOK)?

You stay in full control of your AI spend and data residency, and you can pick the provider you trust. Your key is stored encrypted per-installation in Atlassian Forge storage and never reaches the browser.

What about data privacy?

EstimAI runs on Atlassian Forge and operates no AI servers of its own. When you run an estimate, the content you choose is sent to your configured provider (OpenAI or Anthropic) under your key, and is not retained after the session. No training on your data. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

Which AI models are supported?

OpenAI GPT-4o mini and Anthropic Claude Haiku — switch in Settings anytime.

Which platforms does it work with?

Jira Cloud only, across all project types — team-managed, company-managed, Scrum and Kanban.
